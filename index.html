A Time In History

a time in history. as a Black person existing in this world, it is crucial that I'm aware of my past, feeding my present and at all times always stay in control of my future. revisiting my biases on race, gender, culture and sexuality and continually discarding the parts of my evolution that has been created from a tired history of colonialism and oppression. I have to stay aware and never stop dissecting parts of my humanity that is still being influenced by anti-blackness, anti-me. This piece is a physical manifestation of my desire to dismantle euro-centric standards and maybe unlearn the failures and embrace the kindness.
